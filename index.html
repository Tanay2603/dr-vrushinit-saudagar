Who We Are

A Legacy of Healing & Trust

Dr. Vrushinit Saudagar is a Fellow Homeopathic Psychiatrist and leading Classical Homeopath practicing in Nashik, Maharashtra. He holds a Fellowship in Homoeopathic Psychiatry (MUHS), conferred by former Governor Shri Bhagat Singh Koshyari, and is the first in Nashik district and North Maharashtra to achieve this distinction.

Widely known as a 'Mental Health Survivor' in the Nashik region, he represented India at the AYUSH International Homoeopathic Conference, Dubai (Jan 2024), presenting research on Tourette's Syndrome. A disciple of Dr. Rajan Sankaran and Dr. Mahesh Gandhi, and a follower of the PEM Method, his research focuses on OCD, ADHD, Tourette's Syndrome, Autism, and child behavioural disorders. He is also a classical singer, calligraphy expert, Yoga Shikshak, and columnist for Maharashtra Times (HOMEOHEALING column).
